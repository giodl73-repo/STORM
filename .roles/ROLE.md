# STORM Review Organization

STORM reviews protect weather, seasonal hazard, disaster, preparedness, exposure,
downstream-handoff, and recovery-window records without turning STORM into a
forecasting engine, emergency-advice repo, map model, economy model, ecology
simulator, ritual adjudicator, or game mechanics system.

## Panel

| ID | Role | Lens |
|---|---|---|
| S-1 | Weather Systems Steward | Weather kind, seasonality, signals, and timing |
| S-2 | Hazard/Exposure Analyst | Hazard event, exposure, consequence, and affected systems |
| S-3 | Preparedness/Recovery Reviewer | Preparedness tradeoffs, recovery windows, and downstream decisions |
| S-4 | Consumer Boundary Skeptic | Keeps PORTO, CERES, FAUNA, FLORA, RITE, CANON, LUCIA, and BANISH work out |
| S-5 | Evidence Custodian | Source refs, anchors, validation, and pack readiness |

## Review order

1. Use the panel for weather/hazard schema changes, scenario packs, and consumer
   fixtures.
2. Use the Consumer Boundary Skeptic before accepting map, economic, ecology,
   ritual, identity, interpretation, or gameplay behavior.
3. Use the Evidence Custodian before publishing weather/hazard packs.
