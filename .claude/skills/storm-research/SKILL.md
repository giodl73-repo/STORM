# STORM Research

Use this skill before standardizing hazard contracts, weather terminology,
preparedness categories, source custody, or downstream adoption.

## Workflow

1. State the research question and the decision it informs.
2. Inspect local evidence first: README, fixtures, tests, rubric, and dependency
   trackers.
3. Check external sources only when needed for terminology, formats, or
   weather/hazard claims.
4. Separate weather signal, exposure, preparedness, recovery, and downstream
   handoff instead of flattening hazards into generic disasters.
5. Record findings with citations, implications, confidence, and non-goals.
6. Split recommendations into adopt now, prototype behind a boundary, and defer
   or reject.
